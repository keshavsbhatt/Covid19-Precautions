HOW TO STAY SAFE FROM COVID-19

~ Made With Love By Keshav Bhatt

Wash Your Hands Properly

Regular hand wash with soap or sanitizer for 30 seconds will help you avoid germs.

Cover Your Mouth And Nose

Cover your nose and mouth while sneezing. Even better use a mask

Stay Home Stay Safe

Avoid travelling or being in a crowded place. Stay indoor.

Eat Healthy

Avoid consuming junk food. Eat as healthy as possible and boost your immunity.

Stay Calm

Regular exercise and meditation would help you keep yourself calm and composed.

BE AWARE. BE SAFE.
